User groups who meet @ 801 Labs

DC801

DC801 is the local DEF CON user group that meets every 2nd Friday at 801 labs. Presentations from DC801 focus on information and general security.


SLC Linux

SLC Linux is a bi weekly user group that meets to talk about the Linux operating systems.


Utah Smash

UtahSmash is utahs own smashs brothers competitive tournements, This group is for anyone in Utah, or anyone visiting Utah, who plays smash competitively, as well as those who are just visiting and want info on tourneys, fests, and more. UtahSmash meets every Wednesday starting 6 PM
